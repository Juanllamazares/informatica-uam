Online Colleges RI-Cities

Online colleges in Rhode Island are located across the state. Some of the most popular cities include Providence, RI; Newport, RI; Cranston, RI; and Pawtucket, RI.

Online Colleges in RI-Popular Online Degrees

Some of the most popular online degrees from colleges in Rhode Island include online business degrees, online education degrees, online criminal justice degrees, and online nursing degrees.

Rhode Island Facts

Rhode Island became the 13th state on May 29, 1790. Rhode Island is the smallest state at 37 miles wide and 48 miles long. The official name of Rhode Island is he State of Rhode Island and Providence Plantations,referring to the fact that most of the state is actually on the mainland (Providence Plantations) and only a small portion is an island (Rhode Island).
