Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park Admission Policy:

Registered hotel guests receive passes to the water park as part of their room fees. A limited number of day passes are available for the general public as well.

Directions to Valley of the Spring Resort:

The actual address is 8505 W. State Road 56 in French Lick.

From Evansville: I-164N to I-64E. Take Exit 72, IN-145 toward Birdseye/Bristow. In French Lick, turn right onto IN-56/Indiana St. to resort.

From Louisville: I-64W onto US-150W toward Greenville/Paoli. Left onto US-150/E. Main St/IN-56 to resort.

From Indianapolis: IN-37S to US-150/IN-56 to resort.

Indoor Water Park Features:

Bowl ride, lazy river, tube slides, body slides, interactive play structure with dump bucket, activity pool, toddler pool, whirlpool spa.

Big Splash Adventure Indoor Water Park at Valley of the Springs Resort Overview:

At 40,000 square feet, Big Splash Adventure is a medium-sized indoor water park. Its most prominent feature is its all-glass exterior. The translucent walls and roof allow sunlight in all year long and give the park an open ambiance. And the retractable glass roof is opened in warmer weather to really give the park an open feeling. At the time it opened, Big Splash Adventure was the largest indoor water park with a retractable roof in the United States. Park features include a bowl ride as well as the basic water slides, a lazy river, and an interactive water play station.

The hotel offers over 150 rooms, including suites and standard guest rooms. In addition to the indoor water park, the resort offers an outdoor pool, arcade, buffet restaurant, and meeting and conference areas. It also features The Ranch, a working ranch, with horseback riding, fishing, and other activities available, as well as The Mansion, an upscale guest house with a gourmet restaurant.
