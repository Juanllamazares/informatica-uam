Welcome to Find a Toilet

We are the UK's largest and ONLY database of public toilets.
Wherever possible we list the postcode, opening times, facilities and directions to every toilet in Great Britain, and these details are all available on our mobile phone website www.findatoilet.mobi

About our database

It's taking a long time but so far there are 3700+ published in our database and although you won't find all of the UK's toilets listed here yet, we are adding to them daily so it'll only be a short while before you will.

Contribute
