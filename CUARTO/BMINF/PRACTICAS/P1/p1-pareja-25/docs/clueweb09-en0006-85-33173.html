I.       THE FRENCH LICK RESORT AND WEST BADEN SPRINGS HOTEL

The French Lick Resort is a luxurious retreat and gaming resort located in the quaint Midwestern town of French Lick Springs, Indiana. The French Lick Resort has returned gaming to the Springs Valley for the first time since French Lick Resort closed in 1949.
The historic West Baden Springs Hotel, which is a one mile shuttle ride from the French Lick Resort, is sometimes referred to as the "eighth wonder of the world". The hotel reopened in 2007, after being closed since 1932. The extensive, multi-year renovation project was designed to preserve the hotel's original features and elegance. It has what was once the world's largest free span dome, soaring 110 feet above the atrium floor.

The French Lick Resort has 443 luxurious guest rooms, a fitness center and spa with 24 treatment rooms, classic Pluto Mineral Baths, and multiple restaurants and bars. The West Baden Springs Hotel has six circular stories containing 246 rooms, including eight specialty suites, three historic suites decorated with period furniture, and a lavish Presidential Suite, all surrounding the atrium. The resorts feature a golf course, shops, restaurants, indoor pools, and a world-class spa and fitness center. French Lick Springs is truly the Midwest's premiere resort and casino destination.

The French Lick Resort supports responsible gaming and participates in the Indiana Gaming Commission's Voluntary Exclusion Program (VEP). The French Lick Resort Casino and West Baden Springs Hotel's founders have been recognized for their programs supporting community philanthropy.

Our dedicated staff is committed to providing an unsurpassed experience for every one of our guests. We strive to deliver outstanding service to you in beautiful and luxurious surroundings, and hope that you enjoy our facilities and your visit with us.

Non-Personal Information/Cookie Technology

"Non-Personal Information" is information about you or your activities through which you cannot be personally identified. Like most interactive websites, our websites utilize a website technology known as "cookies." A cookie is a small file that contains information sent by a website that is saved on your hard drive by your computer's browser. Cookies store information that a website may need to personalize your experience and to gather website statistical data, such as which web pages you visit, what material you download, your Internet provider's domain name and country, and the addresses of websites you visited immediately before and after visiting a particular website. Our websites use cookies to collect and store your preferences for viewing our site. The cookie information may also be used to detect and resolve website problems and to assist with customer support concerns.

In addition, our websites use cookies to provide a unique identifier to your computer. This helps us to generate statistics regarding use of our websites, such as the percentage of repeat visitors. In situations where our resorts have Personal Information about our website visitors, the unique identifiers may be matched with Personal Information. In addition, when you book a reservation online to stay at the French Lick Resort and West Baden Springs Hotel, the reservation or confirmation number may be stored in a cookie on our computer and in an order file generated by third party vendors that assist in processing and fulfillment of reservations.

To browse the Internet and our websites without using cookies, you can configure your browser to reject all cookies or to notify you when each cookie is offered so that you can accept or reject each cookie. However, if you reject all cookies, you may not be able to conduct some transactions or use some services on our website and other Internet sites.
