Enrollment Center

The Enrollment Center is located in Building 2, Room 200, on the Salem campus. Office hours are 8 a.m. to 6:30 p.m., Monday through Thursday and 8 a.m. - 5 p.m. Friday, throughout the year, except during official college holidays.

If you need further information about admissions, please call 503.399.5006 or you may reach us by e-mail. Our mailing address is:
