What is Kaboodle?

Kaboodle is the largest and fastest growing social shopping community where people discover, recommend and share products. Kaboodle's powerful shopping tools allow people to organize their shopping through lists and discover new things from people with similar style. With over 12 million unique monthly visitors, Kaboodle is a fun and engaging community of people who love to shop.

What you can do on Kaboodle
» Add what you want from any site
» Create shopping lists
» Express your own style
» Discover new things every day
» Connect with Kaboodlers who are passionate about shopping

Click the Kaboodle Button

See an item you like on another website? Simply click the Kaboodle Button to automatically take a snapshot of the product, its price, and any other relevant information. Then add the product to any of your lists.
