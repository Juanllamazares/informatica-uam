Bathroom Toiletry Wipe Aids

including the Bottom Buddy toilet tissue aid,
and other disability devices for toilet wipes

The Bottom Buddy is a toilet tissue aid designed for self-cleansing when reaching is difficult. The soft, flexible head grips any tissue or pre-moistened wipe securely. Soiled paper or wipes are released into the bowl with a push of the button located in the curved handle.

The Bottom Buddy and other bathroom toiletry wipe aids (also called disability devices for toilet wipes) shown below are comfortable and easy to use hygiene aids that help people who have difficulty bending, or who have limited use of their hands and arms, to function independently when using the toilet.
