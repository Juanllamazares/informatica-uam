Hailing from the Lone Star State, Salt Lick Bar B-Q now makes its mark in Las Vegas as it kicks up tasty western classics like ribs, brisket, pulled pork and of course, its world-famous cobbler, while Blue Plate specials are back and better than ever at the Turf Grill near the Race & Sports book and Poker room.

This 70-acre resort hotel showcases the 35,000-square-foot, Asian-inspired Red Rock Spa, an ultra-modern retreat complete with a state-of-the-art fitness center, salon, movement studio, boxing ring and lap pool.

Whether you're an early riser or stay-up-all-night, Red Rock's luxurious accommodations offer a spacious retreat from the action and excitement. This resort offers 812 guestrooms spanning 20 floors.
