Introduction

Grand Victoria Casino & Resort By Hyatt Rising along the shore of the mighty Ohio River in scenic Rising Sun, Indiana, is a spectacular resort casino...in true Hyatt tradition. Step aboard our 322 ft. long, 40,000 sq. ft., cruising casino...all ship-shape in the style of a Victorian sternwheeler. You'll find over 1,300 slot machines bursting with coins and ready to pay-out.There are 80 table games to challenge you and reward you. Enjoy easy floor-to-floor access with our convenient escalator system. In addition, there are generous amounts of smoke-free areas and plenty of free parking just steps awayfrom the Casino. Recently opened is a spacious and beautiful 300-acre resort with a 200-room hotel that overlooks the tranquil river and an 18-hole golf course. The highly themed Victorian pavilion houses a 15,000 sq. ft. multi-purpose facility for banquets, conference and Las Vegas style entertainment. Dining facilities include a steak house, buffet restaurant, sports bar and VIP Lounge.
