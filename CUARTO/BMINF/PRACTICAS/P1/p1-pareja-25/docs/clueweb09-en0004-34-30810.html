FamilyTreeGuide is designed for established genealogists and family historians who have already collected some genealogy data and input it into a computer program. If you're new to genealogy, then we highly recommend that you start by using Ancestry's free "Build A Tree" program, which is geared to helping newcomers build their family tree.

Build A Tree at Ancestry

Family Tree Guide is a quick, simple and free way for you to share your family history. Within minutes, you can have a dynamically driven website that creatively portrays your family tree. Invest a little more time, and you can add pictures, stories, gravestone photos, and more to bring your family history alive. All for free!
