Family Tree Guide

Family Tree Guide is about convenience.

Placing your family tree online with your own website and website address could never be simpler then what we offer. Our goal is to make this as easy for you as we can. All work that involves the website setup and maintenance will be handled by one of our capable staff, leaving you to handle only the research into the genealogy for your family. Because your family tree is web-based you will be able to access and edit your genealogy from anywhere in the world. And we do all of this for free!

Family Tree Guide is about choice.

With each website you have the ability to provide visitors unlimited access to your genealogy, or restrict their access. You decide! Enable users to view information on living people, add, edit and/or delete from your family tree. Enable a user to download your GEDCOM. Or don't allow any of it! The choice is yours!

Family Tree Guide is about collaboration.

Sharing is the key to success for any family tree. Wouldn't it be much easier if you had 10 people helping you research a line in your genealogy, then just yourself? Our free website provides you the opportunity to share with others, and have them share directly back with you!

Family Tree Guide is about control.

With the advent of wide spread online access and the invention of the GEDCOM standard, a researcher ability to control their genealogy online has become a paramount concern. Who is viewing your family tree? Who is using your family tree? Our free genealogy software takes your family's privacy seriously, and we provide you the tools to protect that privacy!

Family Tree Guide is Free

Family Tree Guide is a quick, simple and free way for you to share your family history. Within minutes, you can have a dynamically driven website that creatively portrays your family tree. Invest a little more time, and you can add pictures, stories, gravestone photos, and more to bring your family history alive. All for free!

Of course the best way to get familiar with Family Tree Guide is to sign up for our free account and start playing around with our rich set of features. There's no obligation and your account will never expire. We believe you will be completely satisfied with your free account and the ease of having your family tree online.
