Area Lodging

Stay and play until your heart is content! Nearby accommodations in the rolling hills of southern Indiana will provide a truly warm and inspiring overnight experience for your Winter getaway. Play for a day or stay for a while! Enjoy the atmosphere of the many local bed & breakfasts, cozy cabins, resorts and motels.

Midweek Packages
  • Stay at one of our local accommodations at least 2 nights and ski or snowboard 3 days for $49.  Children 6 & under receive a FREE Snowpass.
  • Tickets are valid for skiing or snowboarding from Sunday 3pm through Wednesday 9:30pm. Excludes Holiday Week or special Holidays.
  • Excludes December 24, 2008 through January 1, 2009 and January 17, 18 & 19 and February 14, 15, & 16.  All rooms quoted per night based on double occupancy.  Tax & gratuities extra. All prices subject to change without notice.
