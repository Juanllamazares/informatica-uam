BECOMING A MEMBER

The member institutions listed below pay to post their job openings with StudentAffairs.com.

The cost for this service, shown here, is based on Full-Time Enrollment (FTE) and allows the member institution to post an unlimited number of job opportunities.

  • $150 per year for institutions with FTE up to 3000
  • $200 per year for institutions with FTE between 3000-6000
  • $225 per year for institutions with FTE between 6000-10,000
  • $295 per year for institutions with FTE over 10,000

As always, browsing and responding to position listings remain FREE to job seekers!

To become a member institution, please fill out our
Registration Form
or call toll-free at 1-888-757-1997.
