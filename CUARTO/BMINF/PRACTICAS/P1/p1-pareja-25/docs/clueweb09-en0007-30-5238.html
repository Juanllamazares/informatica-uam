Toilet Plunger & Toilet Paper Funny Video

Posted by Chan Kelwin in Just For Laughter

There has been so much hype about this video circulating on the Internet where a group of ladies were having so much fun racing each other equipped with toilet plunger and toilet paper.

I did a quick search and found the video on YouTube. There are a few copies out there with titles such similar to The Hen’s Party. Anyway, I believe the original copy could be the one titled Wild Women of Whippoorwill submitted in May 2008 with over 3 million views to date.

Wow! That is a lot of laughter!

It is not surprising at all seeing the way the ladies handled the stick between their legs. They find it hard to walk, let alone run. However, the hardest part for them was probably trying to poke the stick through the toilet paper hole without using their hands.

I do not want to sound sexist but I am confident a man would have handled his a stick between the legs much better compared to the ladies. Thankfully, I managed to find another YouTube video to back up my statement.

See how good the guy is with the stick between his legs? He had it pointing straight and slightly lifted from the start to the finish. His stick did not sway left-right or up-down much. He even managed to ride off with the toilet paper roll on his stick to the bike. WOW!

Let’s face it. When it comes to doing things with a stick between the legs without using the hands such as running and poking through a hole, we guys win hands down.

This Pole in the Hole Race looks like a fun party game. Now, who wants to bend over and wait for me at the end of the line?

Cheers.
