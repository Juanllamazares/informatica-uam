Annapolis - Mitchell Gallery

The Elizabeth Myers Mitchell Gallery, through exhibitions of museum-quality works, serves the community of St. John's and the wider communities of Annapolis and Anne Arundel County as a resource for viewing, discussing, and learning about fine art. The Gallery's primary purpose is to establish connections between the visual arts and the liberal arts and to promote exhibits of unique historical and regional interest.

Gallery Hours: During the academic year, gallery hours are: Tuesday through Sunday noon to 5:00 p.m. and Friday 7:00 to 8:00 p.m. when exhibitions are in progress. Please note – Gallery hours during the Hammond Harwood House's Goldsborough Family Portraits exhibition are Tuesday through Sunday from noon until 5 p.m.

Docent–led tours are offered every half hour from noon to 3:00 p.m. on Thursdays. No registration necessary.
