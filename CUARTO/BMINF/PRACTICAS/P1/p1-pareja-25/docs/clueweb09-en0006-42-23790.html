Getting Involved at Fairfield University


With so much to choose from at Fairfield University, you'll grow academically and personally while creating lifelong friendships and memories.

Try something new:

  • Join some of Fairfield's student clubs
  • Volunteer with Alumni Relations
  • Read about events on campus and find out what's happening today

More than 80% of our students live on campus. They run nearly 100 clubs and organizations, play on 19 Division I athletic teams, organize a massive intramural sports program, join performance groups, and work for an active student government.

Every spring the Fairfield University Alumni Association presents the St. Ignatius Loyola Medal, William J. Kramer '60 Humanitarian Award and Student Achievement Awards to outstanding full time undergraduate seniors who have shown exemplary service to Fairfield University and the community.
