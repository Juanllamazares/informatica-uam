If your college or course cannot be found on this equivalency site, please contact The Transfer Office at transfer@ferris.edu or by calling toll-free 1-800-4-FERRIS. We will assist you with having your course reviewed for possible approval for transfer credit. Please provide 1.) your college's full name, 2.) the course prefix, number and title, and 3.) the dates you attended the course.
