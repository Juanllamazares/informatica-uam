Getting Involved

The majority of students on campus are involved in activities beyond their academic work—and this is a good thing. The undergraduate experience is about getting a good education, but it's also about growing as a person. Very often outside activities carry over into academic work—and vice-versa—in a very positive way.

Activities within the Residence Halls
There are a number of different social and academic activities within the residence halls. Some are student sponsored, while others are arranged by Residence Life staff. Most students participate in at least one of the scheduled activities in the residence halls.

University and Community Activities
There are tons of social, academic, and recreational groups and activities at The University of Iowa. From intramural sports to literary readings to the Greek system to student government, if you're interested in it, chances are there are others on campus who are already involved.
