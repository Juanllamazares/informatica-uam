Four Winds Casino Resort – New Buffalo, MI

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. When did the Four Winds Casino Resort open?

The Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians opened the Four Winds Casino Resort on August 2, 2007.

Q. What is the age requirement to play in Four Winds Casino Resort?

You must be at least 21 years of age. Please play responsibly.

Q. Does the Four Winds Casino Resort have wheel chairs to use or rent while at the casino for older people that can't walk?
Yes, the Casino has wheel chairs avaibable for patrons who require them. Of course if you have you own chair or motorized accomodation, the Casino has no restrictions on its use.

Q. Does the Four Winds Casino Resort have valet parking?

Yes, just drive up the rotunda in the front of the Casino. In addition, there is a well-lit parking lot, a parking garage and an area reserved for tour busses.

Q. What are the Casino hours?

The doors never close.

Q. How many restaurants does the Four Winds Casino Resort have?

There are seven areas that you can relax and have a fine meal or grab a quick snack and head back out to the gaming areas. The Four Winds Casino Resort buffet will feature shrimp and crab legs, daily.

  • Copper Rock Steakhouse
  • Willowbrook Cafe
  • The Buffet
  • Silver Creek Bistro
  • Timbers Deli
  • Grab n Go
  • Last Licks (Ice Cream Parlor)

Q. I heard there is a child care center at the Four Winds Casino Resort, is this correct?

Yes, hourly child care services are available in Kid's Quest is a 10,000 square foot, fully equipped child care center for children 6 weeks to 12 years-old, as well as CyberQuest, a supervised arcade for older kids. Rates are in the $7-8/hr range depending in the age of the child.

Q. Does the Four Winds Casino Resort have a player's club?

Yes, the casino players club card from Four Winds Casino Resort is called "W Club." You can use it on slots and table games earning Instant Slot Credit, special bonus offers and qualify for upgraded tier status. You can also earn valuable points and redeem them for:

  • Instant Slot Credits
  • W Club jackpot alerts
  • Comp a meal at any of our restaurants
  • Merchandise at Four Winds Outfitters
  • Kid's Quest vouchers
You can also manage your "W Club" card account online. The "W Club card" is free and can be obtained by stopping at the Four Winds W Club booth. You must be 21 years of age or older to participate.

Q. Does the Four Winds Casino Resort have an RV parking area?

Yes. But no long-term RV parking (hookups). RVs and Semi-trucks are permitted to mark in the west end of the parking lot – just follow the signs. There is also a designated area for busses.

Q. Can you tell me the address for the Four Winds Casino Resort?

The address is 11111 Wilson Rd., New Buffalo, MI 49117. For an interactive map of the New Buffalo area, click here.

Q. Does the Four Winds Casino Resort Hotel have a workout room and Internet access?

Yes, the Four Winds Casino Resort Hotel does have a workout facility and each room has high speed Internet access.

Q. Is the Four Winds Casino Resort still hiring?

The Four Winds Casino Resort has hired 99% personnel needed operating the casino, restaurants, and hotel. For current openings call the Four Winds Casino Resort Employment Hot Line at (269) 926-5499 between the hours of 9AM to 4PM EST. Also, see the Four Winds Casino Resort Jobs Information page on this site.

Q. How can I contact the Casino?

You can contact the Four Winds Casino Resort at 866-494-6371.

Q. What is the smoking policy at the Casino

The hotel lobby, desginated hotel rooms, certain areas of the gaming floor and the poker room are non-smoking.

Q. Are there any new games at the Casino?

Players a the Four Winds Casino Resort can experience the excitement of three entirely new categories of slot game play from Vhicago-based WMS Gaming. Slots such as Monoply Big Event, Top Gun and Super Money Grab provide unique player experiences and represent the next-generation in casino entertainment.

Does the Casino have a shuttle bus?

Yes. Currently, the shuttle bus makes runs to the area hotels, the municiple marina and the yacht clubs on weekends and holidays from 6:30 p.m. to 1 a.m. There is also a bus makes a run into Michigan City, Indiana and picks up casino guests at the Outlet Mall on the north side of town. This service should expand in the near future.

Q. Are there any busses that run from the Chicago area to the Casino?

Yes. The Four Winds Casino Resort recently announced a new bus service that will shuttle guests to and from several locations in the Chicago metropolitan area to New Buffalo. MORE
