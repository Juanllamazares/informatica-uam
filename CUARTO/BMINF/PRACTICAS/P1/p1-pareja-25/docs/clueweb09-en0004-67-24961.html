Organizations

In keeping with our regional institutional mission, Bridgewater State College provides some technical support to the following organizations. BSC has no control over their web site content. For BSC faculty- sponsored initiatives and special programs, click here.

Connect
A partnership of public higher education institutions in Southeastern Massachusetts: Bridgewater State College, Bristol Community College, Cape Cod Community College, Massasoit Community College, and the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth.
Hall of Black Achievement
A repository of the records or the significant achievements and contributions of people of color.
Massachusetts Hall of Fame for Science Educators
An organization that honors science educators who have made significant contributions to science education in the State of Massachusetts over an extended period of time.
Massachusetts Geographic Alliance
An organization of Massachusetts teachers dedicated to improving geography education in the Commonwealth.
Project Contemporary Competitiveness
Advanced studies, residential, summer program at Bridgewater State College for academically talented students currently enrolled in grades eight and nine.
Writing Across the Curriculum
A program intended to improve student writing and learning, and to help students become fluent in the specific discourse conventions of their major disciplines.
