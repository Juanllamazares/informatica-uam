Grand Hotel Varna Resort & Casino
Grand Hotel Varna Resort & Casino is a new resort consisting of Grand Hotel Varna (5 stars) and 3 satellite hotels Dolphin (4 stars), Lebed (4 stars), Rubin (4 stars), as well as few other hotels, among them is Rusalka (3 stars, recently re-constructed). This new resorts was a part of St.St. Constantine and Elena resort in the past. Now Grand Hotel Varna Resort & Casino is a separated resorts offering luxury hotels and first class services.
The resort is located on 17 North of Varna and only few kilometers South from Golden Sands and Albena.
The resort is a good choice to all holiday-makers looking for luxury stay and high standard services.
