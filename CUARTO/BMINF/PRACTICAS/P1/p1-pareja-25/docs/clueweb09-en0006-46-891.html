Drivers Licenses and Vehicle Registration

The Department of Public Safety, Driver and Vehicle Services Office, is responsible for all driver and vehicle licenses and renewals.

Online services: Renew tabs, change address, check driving record, report a sale, download forms, tax information and more

Additional information: Road tests, license applications, handicap parking, revocations and reinstatements and other services

Office locations: Find a driver's license exam station or Motor Vehicle Deputy Registrar's office

Telephone 651/296-6911

E-mail: motor.vehicle@dot.state.mn.us
 
Check 511 for weather-related road conditions, construction and congestion, via the Web or phone – 24/7.

Video Cameras: Traffic cameras in the Twin Cities Metro Area and many locations in greater Minnesota provide images of traffic and weather conditions. The images are updated approximately every 60 seconds.
