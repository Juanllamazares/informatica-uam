Toilet terror driving officials potty in San Francisco

Posted January 16, 2009 16:01:00
Updated January 16, 2009 16:27:00

The calm of the San Francisco night has been disturbed recently by the sight and smell of burning toilets.

Eighteen portable toilets at construction sites around the city have been set on fire in the past two months.

No one has been hurt, but the blazes have caused about $US45,000 in damage to the portable outhouses and surrounding property, forcing police and fire officials to step up nightly potty patrols.

The fire department's arson task force is investigating, and it is unclear whether just one person is setting the fires.

San Francisco Fire Department spokeswoman Mindy Talmadge said there have been no eyewitnesses.

The blazes have been set inside and outside outhouses, including some that were locked, she said.

"Who knows what motivates people?" Ms Talmadge asked

- AFP
