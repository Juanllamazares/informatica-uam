Ameristar East Chicago History

Ameristar East Chicago opened on April 18, 1997 as the Showboat casino. Showboat Marina was acquired by Harrah's in 2/26/99 and Renamed "Harrah's East Chicago" with the merger of Harrah's and Caesars, Harrah's sold the former Showboat to Resorts International in 2005 who sold the joint to Ameristar in 2007. Whew.


Rooms at Ameristar East Chicago

Ameristar East Chicago has a 15 floor, 291 room hotel on premises. King Rooms are 504 square geet and come with a big tv and seperate tub and shower. Queen rooms have the same amenities but are fitted with two queen beds.


Ameristar East Chicago Restaurants & Dining

Ameristar East Chicago offers 6 dining options - Double Down Diner, The Buffet at Resorts, TJ's Steakhouse, Miller Pizza Co., All Sports Cafe and Lucky Stop.


Lucky Vibes at Ameristar East Chicago

Ameristar East Chicago is one of the largest casinos in greater Chicagoland at 53,000 square feet on four decks. They've fit close to 2,000 slots, 55 table games and a poker room up in this joint. Its hopping!


Casino games at Ameristar East Chicago

Slots (from pennies to $100) Video Poker (including progrressives) Blackjack, Craps, Baccarat, or Let it Ride, Three Card Poker, Caribbean Stud, Let It Ride, Pai Gow, 3-5-7, and a 16 table poker room.
