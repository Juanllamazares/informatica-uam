Inspired and inspiring Montréal business people...

Serving the community!

 

Operation Back to School  gives business people a chance to go back to school to meet with groups of students and inspire them to earn their high school diploma!  Each person must follow their own academic and professional path.  Everyone must find their own way, develop their own passions, and aspire to the job that suits them.  
Everyone has a place in society - they just have to find it - and business people can certainly help the younger generation to do so.

 

Anyone working in the business community wishing to become involved in community action by volunteering services in their field of expertise is welcome to join Business Volunteers. What makes for the exceptional quality of volunteer work done by businesspeople is that it involves a mutually beneficial exchange.  Donating a few hours of work yields a high return.  The energy and time you give these organizations allow them to benefit fully from complementary styles and skills to advance their projects and those of the metropolis.
