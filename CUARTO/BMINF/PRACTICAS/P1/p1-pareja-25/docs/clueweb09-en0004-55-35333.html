A Bed & Breakfast with complete privacy!

Blue Prairie Bed and Breakfast offers one very unique and spacious guest suite that sleeps up to 2 adults. Located in a new farm house and surrounded by 120 acres of native prairie and woodlands, our guest suite provides complete privacy as well as great birding and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Come try a Bed and Breakfast experience like no other. As our guest, you can enjoy as much privacy as you like. The guest suite has its own private outside entrance, so you can come and go as you please. You can also enjoy the spectacular view (with incredible fall foliage) from the private, new outdoor hot tub just outside your door. Take a walk on one of the hiking trails on our property, or relax on your private patio.

Continental breakfast is included and is delivered to your room. (No having to share a table with strangers.) You will enjoy satellite television, an in-room refrigerator and your own private bathroom.

Blue Prairie Bed and Breakfast is a boater friendly Bed & Breakfast. There are 3 Patoka Lake ramps within 5 minutes. We offer free, secure boat parking, and we have outdoor outlets so you can recharge your boat's batteries.

Blue Prairie Bed and Breakfast is close to several great Hoosier National Forest hiking, biking and horseback riding trails. We are also less than a mile from Patoka Lake, Indiana's second largest and most beautiful lake. The historic towns of French Lick and West Baden are just minutes away, and slopes of the Paoli Peaks ski resort are only a fifteen minute drive. Canoeing and kayaking on the Blue River are forty minutes away. Several nice golf courses are a short drive away, and golf packages are available.

Birding Update: There is an Eagle Watch program at the Patoka Lake Visitor Center on Saturday, January 10, 2009. Field trips to the lake will look for resident eagles. Click for more birding & wildlife info.

Spring is a special time at Blue Prairie Bed & Breakfast. The newly renovated French Lick Resort, Casino and Spa and the West Baden Springs Hotel are now open for business. Party hearty, gamble, or go boating at Patoka Lake, then return to the solitude and privacy of your cozy guest suite to restore your soul in your own private hot tub!
