Kruger National Park Safari Lodges (Safari Bathroom/ Toilet Views)

Well what does this have to do with anything.

This is a light hearted look at one of the unique charecteristics of Safari lodges...

A Safari lodge is built in a  private location, with usually a architecural design that lends itself to uniqeness, quaintness, warmth, different.

As part of this design, many of the lodges private bathrooms and toilets are placed in such a way that give the occupant or user, a open look to the bush...remember there are no fences in a Safari lodge, so any animal can come wondering by.

         

Recently at a lodge in a private game park, adjacent to the Kruger National Park,a lodge manager told me of a incident where a lady was taking a bath in a bathroom, that had clear glass all around it, and a elephant came by, and looked in, through the window...
