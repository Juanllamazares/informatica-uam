Great Idea Fair 
One of these many informative and exciting events at this conference will be the Innkeepers Great Idea Fair. Submit a Great Idea for Innkeepers that you have learned in your business that has worked for you and would help others.  The Conference Committee will select the best 15 ideas for presentation at the conference in the Innkeepers Great Idea Fair. Those submitting selected entries will present their ideas "live" at the Conference. Everybody in attendance will be "winners" because of the great, useful ideas. The specially selected "top three" presenters will receive special recognition and prizes. Deadline for submission of ideas is November 15, 2008 to your State Association President or the IBBA office.
 
Birds of a Feather 
This session is intended for innkeepers to meet in small groups with fellow innkeepers with facilities of nearly the same size.  Special focus will be upon issues that the group faces and solutions that may be appropriate. Inns 1-3 rooms, Inns 4-6 rooms, and Inns with 7 or more rooms. What a great way to share ideas and resolutions to the problems at hand.

Murder at the Four Deuces – New Years Eve
 
It's New Years Eve 1924 and the American economy is booming. Employment is high, new inventions and mass production have improved people's standard of living, and life, for many, is good. Prohibition had been created with the intention of providing a healthier, happier, safer, and more efficient America. These are the jazz years, with new music and dances, new and daring fashions; it is a time of hedonistic pleasure for many.
 
Don Wannabe is hosting his annual New Years Eve Party at his speakeasy The Four Deuces and you are invited to attend. The Four Deuces is a lavish joint serving booze, music and fun. The cream and not so cream of society will be here. With gangsters, hit men, politicians, opera singers, molls and many other interesting guests in attendance there are sure to be a lot of big deals being made here tonight. 

At the end when the murderer is revealed and awards presented. Karmen Ailes will present a short workshop on the ins and outs of doing Murder Mysteries at your own B & B.
