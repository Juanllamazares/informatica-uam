The America Reads Honor Roll

The America Reads Challenge acknowledges and congratulates the colleges and universities that voluntarily accepted the America Reads Challenge. From the inception of America Reads in 1997 to June 30, 2000, more than 1,400 colleges and universities accepted the Challenge and participated in reading or family literacy tutoring efforts. As of July 1, 2000 all institutions receiving Federal Work Study (FWS) funds are required to use 7% of the total amount of their funding to compensate students doing community service activities. In meeting the 7% requirement, an institution must ensure that one or more of its FWS students is employed as a reading tutor or in a family literacy project.

In the first three years of the America Reads Challenge, presidents from the following colleges and universities provided leadership by involving their own campuses and assisting with recruitment of other campuses.
