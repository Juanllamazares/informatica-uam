Elvis Jr. Concert
Date: November 15, 2008
Time: 7:00 p.m.
Location: French Lick Springs Resort
Town: French Lick, Indiana
Telephone number: 1-888-My Hideaway or 1-888-694-4332
Website: www.frenchlick.com

Get “All Shook Up” with the King. Put on your Blue Suede Shoes and get ready for an exciting event! This tribute concert to the “King of Rock and Roll”, Elvis Presley as presented by Elvis Aaron Presley Jr. A versatile performer, Presley Jr. brings the songs and sound of the King alive.

Tickets are available at the Hoosier Sugar Company in the French Lick Springs Hotel or by calling Ticketforce at 1.877.840.0457 or by visiting www.frenchlick.com.

The concert will be located in the Windsor Ballroom located on the second floor of the event center at the French Lick Springs Hotel.

For room reservations, packages or more information, interested guests may visit www.frenchlick.com or call 1-888-936-9360.

Date: November 17-22, 2008
Time: 10:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m.
Location: French Lick Springs Resort
Town: French Lick, Indiana
Telephone number: 1-888-My Hideaway or 1-888-694-4332

Find that perfect gift for someone special at the 1st Annual Holiday Arts & Crafts Fair. Browse a tempting array of holiday ornaments, wreaths, candles, one-of-a-kind collectibles and other assorted hand-made arts and Crafts perfect for gift-giving to others or for decorating your home. The fair will be located in the Windsor Ballroom located on the second floor of the event center at the French Lick Springs Hotel. For more information visit www.frenchlick.com.

Christmas Parade
Date: November 23, 2008
Time: 6:00 p.m.
Location: downtown Paoli
Town: Paoli, Indiana

Christmas Parade at Paoli with theme sponsored by the Paoli Chamber of Commerce, come see the lit parade themed Christmas around the world; lit floats along with Santa himself will get you in the mood for the Christmas season. Parade starts at the Paoli High School and continues around the Courthouse square.
