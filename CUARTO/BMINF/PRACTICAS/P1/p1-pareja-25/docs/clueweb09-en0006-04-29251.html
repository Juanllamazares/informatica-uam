Online Colleges South Dakota

South Dakota has many colleges and schools that offer online degree programs that will fit your busy lifestyle. Online colleges, online universities, online degrees, and online courses in South Dakota can help you finish your education while you work or take care of your family. Online courses can be taken from the privacy of your home or office, with little time spent in a classroom. You can also graduate in 2-3 years with an online degree. Some of the online colleges and universities are located in South Dakota largest cities, including Sioux Falls, SD; Rapid City, SD; Aberdeen, SD; and Pierre, SD.

What are some popular majors in South Dakota?

Students in South Dakota are majoring in all types of fields, but some of the most popular majors are: Biological Science, Engineering, History, Social Sciences (Psychology, Sociology, etc.), and Health Services. Some of the most popular degrees from online colleges in SD include online nursing degrees, online business degrees, online education degrees, online associate degrees, online bachelor degrees, online master degrees, and online criminal justice degrees.

Facts about South Dakota

South Dakota was admitted into the Union on November 2, 1889. South Dakota is home to Mt. Rushmore and Badlands National Park. South Dakota has a rich Native American history and is named after the Dakota and Lakota American Indian tribes.
