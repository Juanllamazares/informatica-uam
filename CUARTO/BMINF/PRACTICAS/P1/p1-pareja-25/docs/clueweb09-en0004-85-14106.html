Toileting / Commodes



All of the toileting products offered on the Adaptivemall.com provide support and comfortable functionality. Making this "necessary function" more relaxed for parents, caregivers, and children alike is the primary reason we have selected these specific items.

Choose from Leckey Easy Seat, Snug Seat, Tumble Forms Carrie Potty Seats, and Columbia Medical. Quality of workmanship and a useful solutions for you can be found by exploring this section further.

We know the importance of proper positioning. Our therapists can help ensure that a proper fit is made between your child and the product you are considering. We have an experienced OT and PT on staff to work with you and your therapist.

To start, please click SIZING HELP. Please provide all measurements possible for the best outcome.



Rifton Blue Wave Toileting System

Highly adjustable and changeable toileting system for children and adults with special needs.

OmniReclining Shower / Commode / Bath Transfer Systems

Columbia Medical Omnireclining shower / commode / bath transfer systems for children and adults with special needs.

OmniCommode/Shower Chairs

Reclining Commode/bath and shower chairs for children and adults with special needs.

Carrie Potty Seats

Tumble Forms Carrie Seats in a Toileting Version!

Leckey Toilet Seats

Toilet and shower seats for children ages 2 to 18 years of age or approximately 32(81 cm) to 66(168).

Flamingo Bath/Shower / Potty Chair

The Flamingo Bath / Shower / Potty Chair is a 2-in-1 toilet and bath/shower seat. It will support children and small adults weighing up to 154 lbs. (70 kg). Target user age is 2 years to small adult.

Pediatric Positioning Chair, Leckey Easy Seat

Pediatric adaptive positioning seat/chair available for children ages 6 months to 12 years old.

Swan Shower / Potty Chair

Made by Snug Seat, the Swan Shower / Potty Chair picks up where the Flamingo leaves off... for bigger kids through small adults, Click picture for more info!

Columbia Toileting

Columbia Medical Toilet Supports and Toilet Ring Modifiers... regular supports and hi-back supports, along with splash guards and ring reducers for a full range of child sizes!

Columbia Medical Secure SeatPositioning Commodes

Columbia Medical Commodes have positioning features that are needed for toileting assistance. Will accommodate virtually all children and young adults, and fit over any institutional or home toilet.

Slider Bath/Shower Chairs

Rolling bath/shower chair for individuals with special needs ages 12 years to older adult. Can also be used over a toilet or as a free-standing commode.
