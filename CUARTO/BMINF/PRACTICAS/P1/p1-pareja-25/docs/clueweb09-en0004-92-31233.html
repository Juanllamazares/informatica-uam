Get acquainted with how to discover and preserve your family history. Learn the basics of family history research, how to make sense of and organize the data you collect, and about the impact the computer has had on research and how you can take advantage of it. Also, find out why you have the right to have access to information from government agencies, some of the rules of etiquette for requesting information from others, and how to develop your own code of research ethics.
