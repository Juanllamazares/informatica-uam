Red Rock Casino Resort Las Vegas

Red Rock Casino Resort, owned by Station Casinos, is an off-strip locals casino on 70 acres in Summerlin. Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa is just minutes from Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area where its dramatic red cliffs inspired its name. The resort combines breathtaking views of both the Las Vegas Strip and Red Rock Canyon. Red Rock is home to Rande Gerber's Cherry Nightclub. A 16-screen theater offers something for the whole family. The pool complex sits on 3 acres. There are numerous crystal chandeliers containing more than 3 million pieces of crystal in all shapes and sizes gracing the casino along with stunning art, flooring, and lighting.



 Jason's thoughts - One of the finest examples of modern architectural beauty in a large scale seen in a long time. Fantastic hotel service and the best barbecue I have ever had at the Salt Lick. Red Rock Resort has an incredible pool complex with 6 pools and 2 hot tubs surrounded with hundreds of trees, cabanas, shady umbrellas and king bed size 24" thick cushioned outdoor sunbathing pads perfect for snuggling. Music is piped in underwater, fountains wash over you as you bask in the sun and rest in your cushioned lawn chair IN the water, great food at reasonable prices with typical drinks served pool side. Kelly and I danced for nearly two hours straight in one of the very best clubs in town - Cherry has stunning lighting and sound with a floating dance floor that vibrates right through your legs with pounding decibels. Except for the 1400 square foot Mirage suite I stayed in one year the room here at Red Rock was the finest. Kelly and I were uncrowded as we soaked simultaneously and watched the flat screen TV mounted over the super deep oversized tub. Request the "mountain and pool view room" over the "strip view" and you'll want to sit in the window and stare for hours while neglecting the 60" Plasma TV. Red Rock also provides a free daily shuttle to and from the strip so you can shop and site see.

Kelly's thoughts - Gorgeous. I love the modern design and colors of this casino. I can't stop looking at the chandeliers. I truly loved the mountain view from our room and wouldn't have switched to a room with a view of the Strip in a million years. Cherry was so much fun. The nightclub also has a bar outside by the Cherry pool. In the middle of the week the crowd was mostly over 30. Oh crap, that's me. Everybody was totally into dancing, too. It was a blast watching the people on the platforms dance like Elaine; but it's all about having fun.

Travel Info - Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa is at 11011 West Charleston Blvd.
Exit I-215 onto West Charleston Blvd. It is 10 miles west of Las Vegas Blvd.
