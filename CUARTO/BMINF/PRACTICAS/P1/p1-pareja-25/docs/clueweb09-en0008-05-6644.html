Contrary to recent news articles and broadcasts, the French Lick Winery owns its facility and will remain in its present location in the former Kimball factory behind the CVS Pharmacy in West Baden, IN.


Accommodations vary from a historic resort, cabins, inn, and bed & breakfasts. Below is a list of accommodations located in our county. For an expanded list of accommodations please visit www.exploresouthernindiana.com or www.southernindiana.org or www.flwbcc.com

Bed & Breakfasts, Cabins & Cottages

Beechwood Inn - Located within walking distance of the winery, a historic mansion with a guest house & martini bar too.

Blue Prairie Bed & Breakfast - Located close to Patoka Lake, a spacious guest suite in a new farm house.

Big Locust Farm Bed & Breakfast - Nestled in a country setting just down the hill from Paoli Peaks.

Lost River Inn Bed & Breakfast - A new country home with a large front porch located near Paoli.

Wilstem Guest Ranch - A working ranch with cattle and horses roaming wide open pastures. Varying size and styles of cabins available; ask about special packages.

The Artists Inn and Gallery - Located on Summit Street in French Lick. This restored home has 4 guest rooms

Serendipity Guest House-Unique dor inside this quaint house is part of the charm. Rates based for whole house rental (812) 936-2426

Underwood Inn-Located in the town of Paoli, this 1865 home has 5 guest rooms. (812) 723-4629

A Little Log Cabin in the Woods - A single cabin, romantic hideaway located near Patoka Lake.

he Pinesat Patoka Lake Village - 12 cabins & 1 lodge for rent located 1 minute from the Osborne Ramp at Patoka Lake

Patoka Lake Marina - 7 floating cabins on Patoka Lake include private boat slips.

The Crazyhorse Ranch & Lodge - 2 units located in a beautiful country setting.

The Lost Resort of French Lick - Rustic barn converted into a very neat retreat.

Cottage on the Links - Located across from the Tom Bendelow Golf Course beside the French Lick Resort & Casino.

Ballard Cottage One - An 1917 Arts & Craft Bungalow located in French Lick

Guest Cottage of The Artists Inn & Gallery  -  100 year old arts cottage which sleeps 4-6 people.

Old Town Cottage  -  Located 1 block from French Lick Resort & Casino sleeps 6.

A Big Log Cabin in the Clouds -  Locate near Patoka Lake, a restored 1863 tavern located on 120 acres.

Resorts & Condos

French Lick Springs Hotel - Historic resort with casino with 443 rooms & suites.

West Baden Springs Hotel - National Historic Landmark Hotel with 247 rooms & suites.

French Lick Springs Villas - 110 units with a rental program and is owner owned and can be exchanged through exchange program.

Legend of French Lick Resort - The former home of Larry Bird turned into a 12 acre retreat.

Historic Ballard Flats-4 units beautifully furnished and renovated apartments available for three or more days (812) 936-0432

Valley of the Springs Resort - Opening in late 2008, this hotel will also have the Big Splash Adventure indoor waterpark!

Motels

Lane's Motel - 43 ground level rooms ideal for fisherman and hunters. Located just down from the casino.

Midwest Inn - 20 ground level rooms located near Paoli Peaks.

Michael T's Motel - 10 ground level rooms located two miles from the casino with weekly, monthly, or group rates available.

The Ritz Motel -18 rooms located near Paoli Peaks-ideal for fisherman or hunters visiting Patoka Lake. (812) 723-2530
