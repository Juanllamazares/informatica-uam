TOTO Innovative Toilet Commercials


Japanese toilets are the best in the world in terms of the number of features and options buttons you can adjust to make your experience as pleasurable and hassle free as possible.

TOTO Ltd is a Fukuoka based company that is the world’s largest manufacturer of toilets. The name “TOTO” is an abbreviation of the two Japanese words forming its full name which translates as Oriental Porcelain.
